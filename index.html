CLient ID (required)

The client id to your spotify application. note for implicit grant flow we do not need a secret key

Redirect URI (required)

Where we will be redirected after we click the link. you will need to add this link in your spotify application

Authorization Scopes (optional)

Enter the scopes for what we are allowed to access (seperated by spaces)

Authorization Link

The request we send to spotify authroize endpoint. After you have generated the link, click it to send a GET request.

After you accept or deny permission, you will be redirected to the redirect uri
